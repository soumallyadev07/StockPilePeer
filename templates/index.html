Trading Purpose

We don't recommend to use our app for trading purposes as we or our ML Model is not a financial expert or investment analysts so we recommend you to use your own intelligence for trading purposes as the app for recreational purposes

Buying & Selling Purpose

Please don't use the predictions for Buying and Selling purposes as the prices of stocks depend on a lot of factors such as profit/loss of the company, growth of the company, market cap, economical conditions etc. which we DO NOT Consider and we use just the historical data for the same.

Accuracy

We don't vouch for 100% accuracy of our model as each time the app is used, the ML model is made and used so the accuracy is diffirent each time but, as per our trials, we have achieved just 1% - 3% error rate.

Monetary Gain/Loss

StockPile Peer does not claim any profit(if any) from the user while at the same time, we do not share any loss that may have caused as Stocks are subject to market, or systematic, risk. This is because there is no way to predict what will happen in the future or whether a given asset will increase or decrease in value. Because the market cannot be accurately predicted or completely controlled, no investment is risk-free.
